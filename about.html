About

Born in 1998, Argentina, Tomás A. Sánchez always showed interest for technology and computers. Once he finished school, he decided he was going to study engineer. Since 2017, Tomas has been studying information systems engineering at Universidad Tecnologica Nacional, Facultad Regional Buenos Aires where he discovered his passion for programming and mathematics and physics.

Nowadays, he is in his junior year of engineering, and looking for start his career in the private sector, where he could grow as a proffesional.

Tomás is not only interested in computers, but in music, being a huge fan of Arctic Monkeys; in literature; films being his favourite directors Quentin Tarantino and Christopher Nolan; and sports.
